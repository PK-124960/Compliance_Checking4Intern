# LLM Model Selection - Research Citations

This document provides research paper citations used to justify the LLM model selection for the thesis.

- **Authors**: Jiang, Albert Q., et al.
- **Date**: October 2023
- **arXiv**: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.06825>

### 2. Mixtral of Experts (8x7B)

- **Paper**: Mixtral of Experts
- **Authors**: Jiang, Albert Q., et al.
- **Date**: January 2024
- **arXiv**: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.04088>

## Key Quotes for Thesis

### On Mixtral Performance (arXiv:2401.04088)
>
> "Mixtral matches or outperforms Llama 2 70B, as well as GPT-3.5, on most benchmarks. In particular, Mixtral outperforms Llama 2 70B on most benchmarks we tested, and it does so with **5× fewer active parameters** during inference."

### On Phi-3 Efficiency (arXiv:2404.14219)
>
> "Despite its compact size, phi-3-mini achieves a level of language understanding and reasoning ability that rivals much larger models."

### On Llama 3 Classification (arXiv:2407.21783)
>
> "Llama 3 models show improved performance on a wide range of benchmarks including question answering, reading comprehension, and classification tasks."
